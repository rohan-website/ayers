A Westover Original

Ayers has been part of Arlington’s business landscape for generations, with a history that explains why the store still feels personal.

1948

Founded by John W. Ayers

The World War II veteran opened J. W. Ayers Five and Ten Cents Store on Washington Boulevard.

1960s

The Mayor of Westover

Ayers supported schools, scouts, civic groups, sidewalks, streetlights, and neighborhood events.

1977

Continued Locally

The Kaplan family bought the store from the Ayers estate and kept its quirky, practical spirit alive.

2025

Readers’ Choice Favorite

ARLnow readers ranked Ayers as a top Arlington hardware store for selection, quality, expertise, and service.
